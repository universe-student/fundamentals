# The Hook

There is no such thing as a free meal; there is only bait. **A bird that is fed every day eventually forgets how to fly.** If you depend on a giant machine for your bread, the machine owns your soul. The moment you disagree with the owner, the bread disappears. Dependency is just slavery with a nicer name.
